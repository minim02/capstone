Welcome to my Capstone Portfolio

Welcome to my capstone portfolio, a digital space showcasing my growth as a writer across creative, academic, and professional genres. This portfolio shows how my writing has evolved through revision, reflection, and experimentation with multiple modes of communication. As Rijlaarsdam, Couzijn, and van den Bergh (n.d.) note, revision is "an intentional, goal-directed activity, operating on different objects (already-written text, text formulated in the mind before being written), at different moments in the writing process," and revisiting my earlier work has allowed me to refine clarity, tone, and rhetorical purpose. By presenting work in both text and digital formats, I aim to show the versatility of my writing and the ways in which reflection and revision contribute to professional and creative growth.
